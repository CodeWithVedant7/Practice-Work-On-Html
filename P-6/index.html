Albert Einstein

Born: 14 March 1879, Ulm, Germany

Died: 18 April 1955, Princeton, USA

Known for: Theory of relativity, E=mc²

Introduction

Albert Einstein was a famous scientist known for his theory of relativity and his equation, E=mc². He made many contributions to physics and won the Nobel Prize in 1921.

Early Life

Einstein was born in Germany in 1879. He was a curious child and loved mathematics and science. He later studied physics in college.

Achievements

  • Theory of Relativity
  • Photoelectric Effect
  • Nobel Prize in Physics (1921)

Later Years

In his later years, Einstein moved to the United States and worked on various scientific projects. He also supported peace and civil rights.
